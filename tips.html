Tips to Stay Safe Online

Use Strong Passwords

Always use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters in your passwords. Avoid using personal information.

Beware of Phishing Emails

Never click on suspicious links in emails. Always verify the sender and hover over links to check their authenticity.

Use a VPN on Public Wi-Fi

When using public Wi-Fi, always use a VPN to encrypt your data and protect your privacy.

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your software and operating system to patch vulnerabilities and stay protected.

Check Your Privacy Settings

Review your social media privacy settings to control who can see your personal information.
